Baked Potato Soup

Description

This baked potato soup is thick, creamy, and an excellent way to use leftover baked potatoes.
It's a family favorite in our house when it's cold and dark outside.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Flour and milk
  • Baked potatoes
  • Green onions
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese and sour cream
